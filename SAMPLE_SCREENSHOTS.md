# Sample Screenshots Layout

## Login

Centered authentication panel with email/password fields, a clear submit button, and navigation to registration.

## Dashboard

Two-column desktop layout:
- Left: large dark source editor with character count, language selector, and submit action.
- Right: review output panel with score, bug/security/performance sections, best-practice notes, and refactored code.

Mobile collapses to a single-column editor followed by the result panel.

## History

Search bar at top, followed by review cards showing language, score, date, findings, refactored code, explanation, and delete action.

## Admin Analytics

Three KPI cards for total users, total reviews, and most reviewed language. Below them are language distribution bars and a compact last-7-days activity chart.
